关于 / About

空间指南是一家关注本土文化、在地性、环境语境与社会参与的独立设计事务所，强调空间、创意与记忆之间的深度对话。
Space-Guide is an independent design studio dedicated to local culture, contextual thinking, and community engagement through spatial and creative practices.
